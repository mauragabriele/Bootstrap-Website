CAFFEINE IN HARRISONBURG

Where to find your best friend in the friendly city?

Start scrolling!



Why coffee?

As a college student who tries to be involved in as many opportunites throughout my four years as possible, I have become very dependent on one of my best friends, caffeine. Getting a good coffee to start off your day can make all the difference, like a fortune cookie. After spending 3 caffeiene hopped years here at JMU, I have definetly acquired quite a roster of coffee/ caffeine hubs. This page gives you the opportunity to find exactly what ambiance or fuel you need without wasting your very precious time. Enjoy!!

Quaint hometown:

If you're looking for the whole in the wall coffee shop where you are unlikely to run into friends or peers in order to lock in on an assignment due tonight, or just reflect on the meaning of life, these are some great breakfast nook kind of places.



Cinnamon Bear Coffee and Bakery - 600 University Boulevard, Harrisonburg VA







Coffee Hound - 64 South Mason Street, Harrisonburg VA






Heritage Bakery - 212 S Main St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Busy Bee Mentality:

This is the place where you can run in and out without all the small talk, whether you're running late to class, or just have the voice in your head repeating your to-do list, these are some great options for your most abrasive days.




Biggby Coffee - 1925 Stone Spring Road, Harrisonburg VA







7 Brew Coffee - 1746 East Market Street, Harrisonburg VA







Merge Coffee Co. - 821 Mt Clinton Pike Suite A, Harrisonburg, VA


Spill the Beans:

For the times you want to sit around a table and brainstorm with a group or gossip with your best friends, these are some of the best options. Come here and sit around the perfectly round tables with lounging chairs ready to spill the coffee beans. After all this is the friendly city, why not have some hubs for your friends?



Broad Porch Coffee - 47 Court Square, Harrisonburg, VA







Greenberry's Coffee Co.- 400 South High Street, Harrisonburg VA







Black Sheep Coffee - 80 East Market Street, Harrisonburg VA
